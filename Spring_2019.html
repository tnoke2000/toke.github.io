CALCULATOR POLICY

Calculators are allowed during recitations. However it will be beneficial to also learn how to work out problems without calculators since they are not allowed on quizzes and exams.

RECITATION ASSIGNMENT MAKE-UP POLICY

All recitation assignments are due on the day of recitation. If you miss a recitation due to illness or any other university approved reasons, you must email me no later than a day after the day you missed a recitation. Upon approval, you have up till Wednesday 5pm for Monday recitations and Friday 5pm for Wednesday recitations.
