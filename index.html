Tolulope Oke

Research interests

  • ❖
    I am interested in Homological and noncommutative algebra, Hochschild cohomology and its associated Gerstenhaber algebra structure and representation theory.
  • ❖
    Other areas of developing interests include invariant theory, algebraic geometry and deformation theory.

  • Bracket structure on Hochschild cohomology of Koszul quiver algebras using homotopy liftings. ArXiv.
  • Cup product and Gerstenhaber bracket on Hochschild cohomology of a family of quiver algebras. Preprint.
